Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the service. This includes:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided at registration
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and notes you enter manually
  • Usage data: basic logs such as login timestamps and page visits, used to maintain service reliability

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data from your device beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and improve Spendly. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Display your expense history and summaries within the app
  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • Identify and fix technical issues with the service

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the service to you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Upon request, we will remove your data within 30 days.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties for their own purposes. We may use trusted third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the platform. These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and are not permitted to use your data for any purpose other than supporting our service.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, analytics brokers, or data marketplaces.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy or this policy, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and transparently.

You can contact us by opening an issue or inquiry through the app. We aim to respond within 5 business days.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
